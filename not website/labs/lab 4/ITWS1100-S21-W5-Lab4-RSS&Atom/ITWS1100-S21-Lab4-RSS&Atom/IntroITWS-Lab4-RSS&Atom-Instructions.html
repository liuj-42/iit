Lab 4: XML

Intro ITWS

Create two XML files, one RSS 2.0 and one Atom 1.0

  • Starting templates:

    • Atom template
  • Example:
    • RSS example
    • Atom example
  • RSS & Atom Resources:
    • RSS Guide (spec)

  • Guidelines:
    • Create five items/entries, each linking to a news or blog posting, or any web content you find interesting (from five different sources). Alternatively, you can make it up if you are so inclined.
    • You may use the same items in both feeds.
    • Post the two files onto the "projects" page of your "iit" website.
    • The feeds must validate.
    • In your review of the formats - take note of the layout of Channels and Items, etc.
    • To look at the feeds in an RSS/Atom reader (found online) or browser. Note: View the feed in your browser after posting it to your website: most browsers have built in RSS and Atom readers, but the feed must be published on a website to be properly rendered. You do NOT need to style you XML - if it looks like plain XML in your browser - this is ok.
